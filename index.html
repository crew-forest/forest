Our Story

Forest Hill High School, Forest Hill JHB

Forest Hill High School is located at Forest Hill, Johannesburg. Our school was established in 1899 to cater for the needs of Forest Hill community and has sine then been consistent in producing A-List Students.

Our Mission

To inspire, as seniors, junior Forestanians with our life long experience in society and workplace.
